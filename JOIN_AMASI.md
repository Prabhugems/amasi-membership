## Document Authentication Flow

1. **Format & size gate** — JPG, PNG, or PDF only, max 5 MB
2. **AI OCR extraction** — Reads and pulls relevant fields from each document
3. **Auto-fill & review** — Extracted data populates your form; you verify before submitting
4. **Admin cross-check** — Admin team manually verifies submitted data against documents

---

## Common Rejection Reasons

- Blurry or glare-damaged scans (AI cannot read them)
- Password-protected PDFs
- Expired MCI/SMC or ASI certificate
- Profile photo with sunglasses, filters, or busy background
- HEIC or WEBP file formats (not supported)

---

## Applicant Checklist

- [ ] MCI/SMC certificate — clear scan, valid (not expired), JPG/PNG/PDF under 5 MB
- [ ] PG degree certificate — legible, showing degree name and university
- [ ] ASI member certificate (LM only) — current, with membership ID visible
- [ ] Profile photo — plain background, no sunglasses or filters, face clearly visible
- [ ] Letter from HOD (ACM only) — on institutional letterhead

## Membership Types

### LM — Life Member

- General surgeon practicing in India or SAARC countries, must be ASI member
- Full membership with voting rights. Can contest elections and participate in policy making.
- **Fee:** Rs.4,230 incl. GST
- **Voting Rights**
- **Required:** MCI cert, PG degree cert, ASI member cert

### ALM — Associate Life Member

- Qualified surgeon, ASI membership not required
- Associate membership. Can convert to Life Member upon obtaining ASI membership.
- **Fee:** Rs.4,230 incl. GST
- **Required:** MCI cert, PG degree cert

### ACM — Associate Candidate Member

- Doctors currently pursuing postgraduate training
- For PG trainees. Becomes eligible for full Life Member upon completing qualifications.
- **Fee:** Rs.4,230 incl. GST
- **Required:** MCI cert, MBBS degree cert, letter from HOD

### ILM — International Life Member

- Surgeons practicing outside India
- For international surgeons interested in minimal access surgery.
- **Fee:** $400 incl. GST
- **Required:** Active license, PG degree cert
