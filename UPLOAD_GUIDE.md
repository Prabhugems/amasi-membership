# Document Upload Guide

Upload instructions for each document required during the AMASI membership application.

---

## MCI / State Medical Council Certificate

**Medical registration certificate**
JPG / PNG / PDF | Max 5 MB

### Do

- Scan the original certificate flat on a surface
- Use good natural or indoor lighting
- Capture the full certificate — all four corners visible
- Resolution: 200 DPI minimum (300 preferred)
- All text must be clearly readable
- Colour scan preferred over black & white

### Don't

- Do not photograph at an angle — causes distortion
- Avoid glare or flash reflection on the surface
- Do not fold or crease before scanning
- Do not crop out any part of the certificate
- Avoid heavy shadows over text areas
- Do not upload a screenshot of a digital PDF

### AI reads these fields

| Field |
|---|
| Full name |
| Registration number |
| Council name |
| Date of registration |
| Validity / renewal date |
| State |

## PG Degree Certificate

**Postgraduate degree certificate**
JPG / PNG / PDF | Max 5 MB

### Do

- Upload original degree or certified true copy
- Ensure full certificate is visible — not cropped
- If multi-page, combine all pages into one PDF
- Text — degree name, university, year — must be sharp
- Scan at minimum 200 DPI
- Attestation stamp/seal must be clearly visible if present

### Don't

- Do not submit marksheet — upload only the degree certificate
- Do not photograph while holding it in hand
- Avoid uploading a partial or zoomed-in image
- Do not submit a password-protected PDF
- Avoid blurred images — retake if text is unreadable

### AI reads these fields

| Field |
|---|
| Full name |
| Degree name (MS / MD / MCh) |
| Specialisation |
| University name |
| Year of passing |

## ASI Membership Certificate

**ASI membership proof**
JPG / PNG / PDF | Max 5 MB | Required for LM only

### Do

- Upload current-year ASI membership certificate
- Certificate from ASI portal (PDF) is the best format
- Ensure membership ID number is fully visible
- Validity period / year must be clearly printed
- If a card — scan both sides if the ID appears on the back

### Don't

- Do not upload an expired certificate (previous year)
- Do not upload payment receipt instead of the certificate
- Avoid screenshots where membership ID is cut off
- Do not upload the ASI life member card if blurred or faded

### AI reads these fields

| Field |
|---|
| Full name |
| ASI membership ID |
| Membership year / validity |
| Category (Life / Annual) |

## HOD Letter

**Letter from Head of Department — ACM applicants only**
JPG / PNG / PDF | Max 5 MB

### Do

- Letter must be on official hospital / institution letterhead
- Must state applicant name, PG year, and department
- Must carry HOD signature and date (within last 6 months)
- Institution stamp/seal must be clearly visible
- Scan the full letter — do not crop the letterhead

### Don't

- Do not upload a plain letter without letterhead
- Do not submit unsigned or undated letters
- Avoid letters older than 6 months
- Do not upload a letter without the institution seal

## Profile Photo

**Passport-size photograph**
JPG / PNG | Max 5 MB | All membership types

### Do

- Recent photo — taken within last 6 months
- Plain white or light-coloured background
- Face clearly visible — front-facing, eyes open
- Passport-size crop: face fills at least 70% of frame
- Good even lighting — no harsh shadows on face

### Don't

- No sunglasses, masks, or hats
- No filters or heavy retouching
- Do not upload a group photo or full-body photo
- Avoid dark or patterned backgrounds
- Do not upload a photo cropped from another document

### AI checks

- Face detected
- Single person
- Front-facing
- Plain background

## General Scan Quality Rules

All documents must follow these guidelines.

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Resolution** | Minimum 200 DPI. 300 DPI gives the best OCR results. Phone camera at full resolution is usually fine. |
| **Lighting** | Scan under even indoor light or near a window. Avoid direct flash — it creates glare that blocks text. |
| **Angle** | Hold your phone directly above the document — not at an angle. All four corners must be visible and unfolded. |
| **File format** | JPG, PNG, or PDF only. No HEIC, WEBP, or Word files. PDF must not be password-protected. |
| **File size** | Max 5 MB per file. If larger, use a free scanner app (Adobe Scan, CamScanner) to compress. |
| **Colour vs B&W** | Colour scan is strongly preferred. B&W is accepted but may reduce OCR accuracy on seals and stamps. |
